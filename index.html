Guiter Type

Acoustic Guiter

An acoustic guitar is a string instrument with a hollow wooden body. A small acoustic guitar, like a parlor or travel guitar, is more compact and easier to carry.

Electric Guiter

An electric guitar is a string instrument that uses pickups to convert string vibrations into electrical signals, which are amplified to produce sound.Common in rock, jazz, and pop music, it often includes controls for tone and volume.

Ukulele

A ukulele is a small, four-stringed instrument from Hawaii. It has a bright, cheerful sound and comes in sizes like soprano, concert, tenor, and baritone. Easy to learn and portable, it's popular in folk, pop, and Hawaiian music.

Acoustic Guiter

A banjo is a stringed instrument with a circular body and a drum-like head. It usually has four or five strings and is known for its bright, twangy sound. Popular in folk, bluegrass, and country music, the banjo is played by plucking or strumming the strings.
